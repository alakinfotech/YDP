Smoking Cessation Program

Welcome to the YDP Smoking Cessation Program. This is what you can expect. First, we provide a process for you to make a plan. It consists of a ten point program. Your plan will be a work in progress. A tracking chart is available to help you in that plan. Second, e-health support will be available to support you with your plan. The most critical time is the first thirty days, particularly the first week so we have devised a program to support your plan during this initial phase. This consists of the following: 1.Daily email reminders on your plan 2.Initial and follow up with your coach should it be necessary This is not an easy task to overcome nicotine addiction but together we can make it happen.
